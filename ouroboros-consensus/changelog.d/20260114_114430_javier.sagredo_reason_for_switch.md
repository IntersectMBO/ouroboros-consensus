### Breaking

- Modify the `SelectionChangedInfo` to include:
  - The `ChainOrderConfig` used during chain selection.
  - Modified the `oldSuffixSelectView` to store the length of the new fragment
    in the cases where it was `Nothing`. It now contains a
    `SelectViewOrBlockDistance`.
- Define `ReasonForSwitch` and `selectionChangedReason` to allow consumers of
  `SelectionChangedInfo` to accurately infer the reason for the switch.

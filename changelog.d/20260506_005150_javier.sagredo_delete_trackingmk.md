### Breaking

- Delete the `TrackingMK` map kind. The few sites that produced a `TrackingMK` now produce a `DiffMK` directly.
  - Removed: `TrackingMK` constructor, its instances (`ZeroableMK`, `CanMapMK`, `CanMapKeysMK`), and the helpers `trackingToDiffs`, `trackingToValues`, `rawTrackingDiffs`, `attachAndApplyDiffs` (and `'` variant), `attachEmptyDiffs`, `prependTrackingDiffs` (and `'` variant), `rawAttachAndApplyDiffs`, `rawPrependTrackingDiffs`, `calculateDifference'`.
  - `calculateDifference` and `rawCalculateDifference` now return `DiffMK` instead of `TrackingMK`.

### Patch

- Made the `Eq` and `Ord` instances for `OneEraGenTxId` (and hence for
  `TxId (GenTx (HardForkBlock xs))`) allocation-free when both ids are from the
  same era. They now walk the era sum in lockstep and use each era's own `Eq`
  and `Ord`, instead of serialising both ids to their raw hash. Cross-era
  comparisons, which only occur around a hard fork, still fall back to the raw
  hash.
